Getting To Know You ♫

The Basics

I'm Jesse, a Web Design student in Austin, Texas. I love to cook, spend time with my dogs and fella, play board games, weave and craft, and learn new things.


The Backstory

I grew up in Cantonement (pronounced Can-TONE-ment), a small town in Florida right outside of Pensacola. I'm an only child and spent my childhood with my pet chickens and cat and imaginary friends.

There's More

I spent a lot of time in the summers and winters with family in Upstate South Carolina and Western North Carolina. There's a small town in NC named after my great-grandfather.

I have another home away from home in central Mississippi. I grew up going to summer camp in a tiny town called Utica. I loved it so much, I was on staff for five years and ended up going to college in Jackson, about 30 minutes away.


So How Did I End Up In Austin?

Last March, my fella and I sold most of our stuff, packed up our cars with what we could fit in them (including our two big dogs), and made the drive to Austin.

But Why?

Opportunities, big city adventure, tacos.


What Makes Me Tick?

I love cooking and trying new recipes with each week's CSA box, from amazing local farm Johnson's Backyard Garden (see also for website that is well designed).

I'm a dog person. I'm a weekly dog-walker at APA! and would most likely rather stay home with my dogs than go out.

I'm curious and love to learn new things. Be it web design, a new weaving technique, or about the Moontower down the street from my house.

Pete Seeger, Rachel Carson, and John Muir are some of my biggest heroes and I want to be a park ranger when I retire.


Why Web Design?

I'm excited to get into a field where constant learning and experimentation/practice is essential. I want to think critically and creatively and explore design specifically. It's also related to my degree, which I am pumped to finally be using out in the world.
